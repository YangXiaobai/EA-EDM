During periods of high energy demand, the grid needs a helping hand. That’s why we’ve launched PowerResponse.*
Be a part of the solution
PowerResponse is an important program, designed to help us understand whether a community effort can help us to improve grid reliability in times of high demand. Our goal is to find new ways to better use the energy we have, and ensure a stable electricity supply.
Here’s how you can get involved
By signing up to PowerResponse, we’ll ask your household to volunteer to reduce electricity usage during a certain time. You’ll get an SMS notification to let you know when you can get involved.
  Up to 20 events per year 1 to 4 hours duration

Plus, you’ll be rewarded

If you participate in a PowerResponse event, you could earn bill credits of $5- $20.^
